About

no idea is a Mythic Cutting-Edge-oriented guild that raids two times a week during Saturday & Sunday mornings to afternoon from 11 AM - 3 PM EST. Established in 2014 around the middle of Siege of Orgrimmar tier. Previously, we've remained the top 2-day guild and weekend guild on Illidan-US up to the Uldir tier in BFA. Since then, we've been raiding on and off however, achieving most Cutting Edge achievements during each raid tier remains a major focus to this day, with the additional goal of increasing our competitiveness against the top weekend and two-day guilds.

Guild Charter

1. Atmosphere

We aim to maintain a focused but relaxed raid environment. Disruptive behavior is not tolerated. All members should treat each other with respect. Constructive feedback is welcomed when communicated respectfully.

2. Efficiency

High attendance is essential. We maintain a roster of ~25 raiders to cover absences and optimize comps. If you plan to step away, please give at least two weeks' notice.
