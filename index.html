About

no idea is a Mythic Cutting-Edge-oriented guild that raids two times a week during Saturday & Sunday mornings to afternoon from 11 AM - 3 PM EST. Established in 2014 around the middle of Siege of Orgrimmar tier. Previously, we've remained the top 2-day guild and weekend guild on Illidan-US up to the Uldir tier in BFA. Since then, we've been raiding on and off however, achieving most Cutting Edge achievements during each raid tier remains a major focus to this day, with the additional goal of increasing our competitiveness against the top weekend and two-day guilds.

Guild Charter:

1. Atmosphere: Our primary focus is to cultivate a relaxed and enjoyable raid environment while simultaneously striving for cutting-edge achievements. We have a zero-tolerance policy for any disruptive behavior. It is highly encouraged that all participants treat each other with the utmost respect while being a part of no idea. Constructive feedback towards the leadership and the raid group as a whole is always welcomed, as long as it is expressed with mutual respect.


2. Efficiency: Maintaining high attendance during our designated raid times is our highest priority. We aim to maintain a roster of 25 individuals to account for any absences and to bring in skilled raiders for progression fights. If you feel that this guild may not be the right fit for you or if you need to discontinue raiding, we kindly request a two-week notice if possible. This will allow us to search for a suitable replacement to cover your position.

Please be aware that occasional rotation of raiders may be necessary. If you are asked to sit out, we expect you to be available to join at any time during our raid schedule. Given our limited raiding hours, there is little room for errors. We request that all raiders arrive on time, fully prepared with consumables, updated addons, and downloaded weak auras, among other necessary preparations.

Furthermore, we strongly encourage our raiders to play the class and specialization in which they feel most comfortable within a raiding environment, particularly during progression. Above all, we expect our raiders to continuously strive for improvement by maximizing their performance logs each week and demonstrating their utmost capabilities.


3. Progression: We have decided not to allocate an additional day for normal or heroic difficulties. Our goal is to maintain maximum efficiency with our two-day schedule. However, we understand that certain dungeon items may significantly benefit specific specializations. Hence, we request that you allocate time before raiding to acquire the item(s) you require to enhance your character.

Additionally, we anticipate our raiders to come well-prepared with knowledge of the boss encounters, ensuring that no one enters the raid unprepared. We will be utilizing RCLootCouncil to vote on gear allocation, prioritizing those who would benefit most from the upgrade.

During progression, we expect our raiders to utilize consumables when necessary, such as saving a potion or DPS cooldown for specific phases of a fight, if required. Moreover, we encourage healers to contribute DPS when healing demands are low. The order of priority during encounters should be Boss Mechanics > Adds > DPS/Healing.



Additonal Guild Info:

  • Check discord daily: This is how we discuss boss strategies and information to our raiders. We expect you to not only read the post but to contribute as often as you can.

  • Post on the #late or absent discord channel: if you have to miss raid: Telling an officer isn’t always adequate.

  • Minimize random AFK’s while we raid: We give a 10minute break halfway throughout the raid day, try your best to save water/bathroom breaks until then. If you do have to AFK please let someone know.

  • Research: Raiders are expected to know the fights along with how to optimize your character. Look at Warcraft logs, Class Discords, and WoWhead.

  • All BoE's that drop in the raid must be traded to the GM or to the guild bank. We will be using Loot council to distribute BoE's that drop to help gear raiders. If a BoE's isn't an upgrade, it will be sold, this helps supply the guild with gold for guild repairs, flask, feast, etc.

  • Trials: The trial period is about 3-4 weekends, or 6-8 raiding days. What we look for is avoidable damage, mechanics, attitude, and attendance.
